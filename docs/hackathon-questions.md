# Ask Your Data — Hackathon Test Questions

Use these questions to test and validate your solution. They cover a range of difficulty levels — from simple lookups and definitions to multi-step analytical queries. Your solution should be able to handle all of them accurately.

1. What is 'Centercode'?
2. What is the average satisfaction rating across all projects?
3. How many feedback tickets are there per type (issue, idea, praise, discussion)?
4. Which 5 projects have the most participants?
5. What is an 'Active Tester'?
6. Which projects have a satisfaction rating below 3.5?
7. Which accounts have participants across the most projects?
8. What does 'Activities' mean in the preview program context?
9. What is an 'Alpha' (aka Internal preview)?
10. What is the ratio of resolved vs unresolved feedback per project for ongoing projects?
11. Which participants have submitted feedback across 3 or more different projects, and what is their average impact score?
12. What does 'Activity Status' track?
13. For each ongoing project, what percentage of participants are active testers?
